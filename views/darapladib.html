Darapladib


How it works:

Inhibits Lp-PLA2, an enzyme that causes the retinal capillaries to become permeable leading to edema.
Lp-PLA2 is upstream of VEGF in DME (Diabetic Macular Edema) pathology, thus treating source, not symptom, of inflammation in DME

Used to Treat: Diabetic Macular Edema

Diabetic Macular Edema (DME) is an accumulation of fluid in the macula—part of the retina that controls our most detailed vision abilities—due to leaking blood vessels.
