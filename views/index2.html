Pipeline

Lead Drug: Darapladib


Used to Treat: Diabetic Macular Edema

  • Diabetic Macular Edema (DME) is an accumulation of fluid in the macula—part of the retina that controls our most detailed vision abilities—due to leaking blood vessels.
  • With the rapid increase of the Diabetic population, DME affects over 1.5 million people and current treatments are insufficient.
  • The current treatments can be dangerous and invasive, and mainly treat the symptoms, however, Darapladib is a non-invasive drug that treats DME at the source
